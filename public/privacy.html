Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

Neon Slide ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Data Collection

We collect device information and gameplay data to provide and improve the App. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you.

2. Data Utilization

  • To save terminal progress (lives, credits, unlocked sectors).
  • To display relevant transmissions (ads) via third-party providers.
  • To improve gameplay mechanics and user experience.

3. External Networks

The App may use third-party services (e.g., ad networks like AdMob, analytics services) that may collect information used to identify you. These services operate under their own privacy protocols.

4. Security

Your game progress is saved locally on your device. Clearing app data will result in a complete memory wipe.
